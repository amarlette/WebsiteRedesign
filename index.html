About Me


I am a busy forth year undergrad Computational Media major at Georgia Institute of Technology. I develop curriculum and teach with Non-Profit STEMPower & am a Lead Student Assistant at GT’s College of Computing Department of Outreach, Enrollment, and Community (OEC). I did research under Dr. Ayanna Howard in the Human-Automation Systems Lab (HumAnS). I am affiliated with the National Center for Women In Technology and Technolochicas and I am always open to speaking about how I got into
the Technology industry.
